How publishing works

1. You fill in the form. Title, domain, Node, abstract, and body. All fields are required except attachment.

2. AI classifies your post. An AI classifier reads your title and abstract and detects the domain. You'll see a strip showing the detected domain and confidence level.

3. Post goes live. If confidence is above 70%, your post publishes immediately into your Node. If unclear, it's held for a brief human review — not rejected.

4. Scholars can review it. Other Scholars in your Node can leave Clarity and Depth ratings. These scores appear on your Folio.

5. Guides can Seal it. If a verified Guide finds your work exceptional, they can issue a Seal — the strongest signal of quality on Rainfont.
